The Seed to Stitch Project

Quietly Crafted.
Deeply Rooted.

Reviving the
Indigenous Soul

Felis Onca was born from a singular mission: to protect the purity of indigenous organic cotton and the hands that weave it. Unlike mass-produced textiles, our fibers are grown from heirloom seeds, nourished by rain, and dyed with the earth itself.

We believe luxury is a responsibility. By ensuring our artisans—the heart of our project—earn 15% above market standards, we are not just making clothes; we are preserving a heritage.
