The Primordial Seed

While the world settled for the fragility of industrial cotton, we went back to the Earth’s first draft. Most 'luxury' today is built on thirsty, genetically modified fibers designed for machines, not skin.

Felis Onca works exclusively with Ancient Old World Cotton—the Gossypium herbaceum and Karunganni varieties. These are rain-fed souls, drinking only what the clouds provide, resulting in a tactile depth that modern hybrids have long lost.

Colors Borrowed
from the Soil.

Our hues are birthed in vats of Indigo, Madder root, and Myrobalan. Using mineral-rich heritage waters, we bind these pigments to the fiber. These are living colors—they evolve with the wearer, developing a patina of life that synthetic dyes can never achieve.
